COMPANY BACKGROUND

Storm Power Events (Pty) Ltd is a diversified Company with vast interest in Events Planning, Hosting and Management, Catering and Décor Services, Artist Promotion and Management Services, Entertainment and Sound Equipment Hire Services. The Company is wholly (100%) South African black-owned. By exploiting the government’s favorable black, youth and woman empowerment policies, the Company seeks to firmly establish itself in the aforementioned areas of business, as well as, empower youths and woman in the communities it does business.

Vision

As a Black Youth and Woman Economic Empowerment Company, we will strive to come to a leading employment creator in the aforementioned areas. Furthermore, we envision our Company being a leader, and pace setter in the domain of Events Planning and Management, Catering and Décor Services, Artist Promotion and Management Services, Entertainment and Sound Equipment Hire Services. We aspire, amongst other areas, to be a household name for the promotion of local artist, events and extent of having signed with major producing companies.

Mission

Our mission is to constantly endeavor to provide affordable and high-quality products and services to our esteemed customers and clients respectively. StormPowerEvents (Pty) Ltd is committed to excellence in satisfying our customers’ needs and strives for quality and safety. Our mission is to provide our customers with a one-stop product and service solutions at the most convenient time and affordable rates
